While 84% of developers use or plan to use AI tools, nearly half don't trust their accuracy. This article outlines three critical risk checks developers should perform before deploying AI-generated code to production: understanding what the code does, assessing what systems it will access, and evaluating its long-term maintainability beyond the initial demo.
